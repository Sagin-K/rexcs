RExCS - β

The Railway Exchange Configuration System (RExCS) is a web-based application developed by Indian Railways to manage its voice requirements.

Indian Railways has adopted an Asterisk-based VOIP solution for its communication needs, which is a software platform used for building communication applications such as VoIP telephony systems, video conferencing, and messaging applications. With Asterisk, Indian Railways can take advantage of a wide range of communication services, including call routing, voice mail, interactive voice response (IVR), and conference calling.

Asterisk is known for its high configurability and flexibility and can be easily integrated with other software applications to create customised communication solutions. The software supports a variety of protocols and codecs for communication over the internet, including SIP, H.323, and IAX.

To simplify the management and configuration of Asterisk-based VOIP Exchanges in Indian Railways, RExCS will offer a graphical user interface.
